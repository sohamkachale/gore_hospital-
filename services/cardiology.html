Looking for the best cardiology hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune? Welcome to Gore Hospital, where expert cardiologists, advanced cardiac technology, and 247 emergency services come together under one roof. Conveniently located near Ravet, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Punawale, Nigdi, and Akurdi, Gore Hospital has earned a reputation as one of the most reliable heart hospitals in PCMC and Pune.

Cardiology Services at Gore Hospital

1. Non-Invasive Cardiology

We offer painless and accurate tests like ECG, 2D Echo, TMT stress tests, and Holter monitoring. These give our cardiologists a clear picture of your heart health, helping detect issues early and guide prevention or treatment.

2. Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive options such as angioplasty, stent placement, and pacemaker implantation restore blood flow, reduce risks, and help you recover faster.

3. Cardiac Surgery & Advanced Treatment

We perform CABG (bypass), valve repair/replacement, and more. Advanced technology and personalized care ensure safe outcomes and long-term heart health.

4. Pediatric Cardiology

Dedicated diagnosis and treatment for congenital and acquired heart issues in childrendelivered with expertise and compassion.

5. Emergency Heart Care

Round-the-clock emergency cardiac services for heart attacks, chest pain, and other urgent conditions with a fully equipped cardiac ICU.
