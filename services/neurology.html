Best neurology hospital in Ravet Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune

Looking for the best neurology hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune? Welcome to Gore Hospital, where expert neurologists, advanced neurology technology, and 24x7emergency services come together under one roof. Conveniently located near Ravet, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Punawale, Nigdi, and Akurdi, Gore Hospital has earned a reputation as one of the most reliable neurology hospitals in PCMC and Pune.

Neurology Services at Gore Hospital

1. Stroke Management

We provide expert treatment for stroke, including clot-busting therapies and rehabilitation to maximize recovery. Our neurologists work round-the-clock to ensure the best outcomes.

2. Epilepsy Care

Comprehensive epilepsy treatment for adults and children, from diagnosis to medication management, with cutting-edge technology to monitor and control seizures.

3. Advanced Neurological Surgeries

We offer neurosurgeries for conditions such as brain tumors, spinal cord injuries, and other serious neurological disorders. Our state-of-the-art operating rooms and skilled surgeons ensure safe procedures.

4. Alzheimer's & Dementia Care

Our clinic provides specialized treatment for Alzheimer's and dementia patients, focusing on early diagnosis, personalized care, and support for both patients and their families.

5. Pediatric Neurology

We treat neurological issues in children, such as developmental delays, epilepsy, and cerebral palsy, providing compassionate care tailored to young patients and their families.
