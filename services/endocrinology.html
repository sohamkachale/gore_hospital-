Best Endocrinology Hospital in Pune | Diabetes & Thyroid Care

At our Endocrinology Hospital in Pune, we specialise in diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide range of hormonal and metabolic disorders. From diabetes and thyroid conditions to pituitary, adrenal, and growth hormone disorders, we provide personalised care that addresses the root cause of the problem.

Our team of expert endocrinologists, supported by advanced diagnostic tools and patient-focused care, works to provide holistic and effective treatment at Gore Hospital. Our mission is to offer holistic, patient-centred care, combining advanced diagnostics, cutting-edge technology, and expert guidance to improve your health and quality of life. We treat each patient as an individual, not just a case, ensuring compassionate support throughout their journey.

Our Endocrinology Services

1. Comprehensive Diabetes Care

We provide complete diabetes management for Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes. Our team monitors blood sugar levels, prescribes medication, and offers lifestyle and dietary counseling. Using modern tools and continuous follow-up, we help patients maintain optimal sugar control and prevent long-term complications.

2. Thyroid Disorders Diagnosis & Treatment

Our experts diagnose and treat thyroid conditions including hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, thyroid nodules, and post-surgical care. Using advanced blood tests, ultrasound, and imaging, we develop individualized treatment plans. Patients receive ongoing monitoring, medication guidance, and education to maintain hormonal balance and overall well-being.

3. Pituitary & Adrenal Disorders

We manage pituitary and adrenal gland disorders with precision. Conditions such as Cushing's syndrome, Addison's disease, or pituitary tumors are assessed using advanced diagnostics. Our specialists design personalized treatment plans, combining medication, lifestyle adjustments, and referral for surgical intervention when necessary.

4. Growth Hormone Deficiency Treatment

Our hospital provides evaluation and treatment for children and adults with growth hormone deficiency. Using hormone testing and growth monitoring, we create personalized therapy plans. Treatments aim to improve growth, metabolic health, energy levels, and overall quality of life through safe, evidence-based growth hormone replacement therapy.

5. Osteoporosis & Bone Health Management

We provide comprehensive bone health care, including screening, diagnosis, and treatment of osteoporosis. Using bone density tests and lab evaluations, we design treatment plans with medications, supplements, and lifestyle counseling. Our goal is to strengthen bones, prevent fractures, and improve mobility and quality of life.

6. Obesity & Metabolic Syndrome Management

Our endocrinologists address obesity and metabolic syndrome through personalized evaluation of diet, exercise, hormonal imbalances, and medications. We focus on long-term weight management, controlling associated risks like diabetes, hypertension, and cholesterol. Regular follow-up ensures sustainable lifestyle changes and improved overall metabolic health.

7. Hormonal Imbalance & Replacement Therapy

We treat hormonal imbalances in adults, including menopause, andropause, adrenal insufficiency, and other endocrine disorders. Our specialists evaluate symptoms, lab results, and lifestyle factors to provide safe and effective hormone replacement therapy. This restores balance, improves energy, mood, and overall well-being.

8. Preventive Health Check-ups & Screening

We offer endocrine screening packages for early detection of diabetes, thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalances, and metabolic conditions. Regular check-ups allow timely intervention, preventing complications and promoting long-term health. Education, counseling, and lifestyle guidance are provided to help patients maintain optimal endocrine health.
