Pulmonology (Chest & Respiratory Care)

Home Pulmonology

Best Pulmonology Hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune

Are you looking for a trusted pulmonologist in Pimpri Chinchwad or a hospital that truly understands lung and respiratory health? You've come to the right place. At Gore Hospital—the Best Pulmonology Hospital in Ravet, Pune—we provide advanced, compassionate care for asthma, COPD, sleep apnea, pediatric lung issues, and more.

Our Pulmonology Services

1) COPD Treatment in Pune

Advanced therapies and pulmonary rehabilitation to reduce flare-ups and improve overall lung function and quality of life.

2) Asthma Management in Ravet

Personalized programs with the latest inhalation therapies, allergy testing, and lung function assessments for better control and fewer limitations.

3) Chronic Bronchitis & Emphysema Care

Tailored treatment to reduce inflammation, improve oxygenation, and ease breathing—combining medication with physiotherapy and pulmonary exercises.

4) Sleep Apnea Diagnosis & Therapy

Sleep studies, CPAP therapy, and lifestyle planning to restore healthy sleep, boost energy, and protect long-term heart–lung health.

5) Pulmonary Rehabilitation Programs

Structured exercise, breathing techniques, and nutrition guidance to fortify lungs and improve endurance for daily activities.

6) Pediatric Pulmonology (Children's Lung Care)

Gentle, child-friendly diagnosis and treatment for asthma, recurring coughs, and respiratory infections—supporting healthy growth.

7) Pulmonary Function Tests (PFTs)

Accurate measurement of lung airflow and volume to detect asthma, COPD, or restrictive lung disease early for precise treatment.

8) Chest Imaging & Lung Diagnostics

Advanced X-rays, CT scans, and high-resolution imaging for faster, more accurate diagnoses of lung infections, tumors, and abnormalities.

9) Sleep Studies for Apnea & Snoring

In-depth monitoring of sleep patterns and oxygen levels with personalized plans to improve sleep quality and daytime alertness.

10) Allergy Testing & Management

Identify and manage environmental/seasonal triggers with prevention, medication, and desensitization therapy.

11) Interstitial Lung Disease (ILD) Treatment

Early detection and personalized ILD care with imaging, medication, and rehab to slow progression and ease symptoms.

12) Tuberculosis (TB) Care & Management

Complete TB diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up in a safe, monitored environment with adherence-based therapy.

13) Bronchoscopy & Lung Biopsy

Minimally invasive procedures to pinpoint infections, tumors, or structural issues and guide effective treatment plans.

14) Pulmonary Rehabilitation for Long-Term Recovery

Guided exercises, education, and breathing retraining to regain stamina, independence, and confidence after treatment.

15) Preventive Lung Health & Lifestyle Counseling

Early detection, smoking cessation, and lifestyle modifications to protect and maintain healthy lungs.
