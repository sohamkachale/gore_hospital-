When your child isn't feeling well, every second feels heavyand finding the right care becomes your only focus. At the Best Pediatric Hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune, we understand that feeling deeply. That's why our mission is simple: to combine expert medical care with the warmth, patience, and understanding every parent hopes for.

Our team of experienced pediatricians, neonatologists, and child specialists is here to support your child's health journey from the very first day. Our Pediatric Services

1. General Pediatric Consultation

Our pediatric OPD in Ravet offers routine check-ups, growth monitoring, and treatment for common illnesses like fever, cold, or infections. Experienced pediatricians guide parents on nutrition, immunity, and preventive care to keep your child healthy and safe.

2. Child Vaccination & Immunization

Our child vaccination center in Ravet provides all recommended vaccines for infants, toddlers, and children as per IAP/WHO schedules. Parents receive guidance on boosters, side effects, and overall protection.

3. Neonatal & Infant Care (NICU)

Our neonatal care hospital in Ravet, Pune offers specialized care for newborns, premature babies, and infants with health challenges. Modern NICU facilities and expert neonatologists ensure vigilant monitoring and timely interventions.

4. Pediatric Emergency & Critical Care (PICU)

The pediatric ICU in Pimpri Chinchwad manages emergencies like respiratory distress, high fever, trauma, and severe infections. With 247 readiness, our team stabilizes and treats critical cases with precision and empathy.

5. Pediatric Surgery & Diagnostics

From minor procedures to complex pediatric surgeries, we ensure child-friendly, safe treatment. Our pediatric diagnostic services cover imaging, labs, and comprehensive peri-operative care.

6. Specialized Pediatric Clinics

Targeted clinics for ENT, orthopedics, physiotherapy, nutrition, and developmental assessment provide focused, ongoing care for holistic growth and well-being.
