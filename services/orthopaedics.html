Best Orthopedic Hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad Pune

Are you struggling with joint pain, back problems, or sports injuries? Finding the right orthopaedic care can feel overwhelming - but Gore Hospital is here to help. Recognized as the best orthopaedic hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, and Pune, we provide expert treatment with a caring, patient-focused approach.

Our goal is to help you move comfortably, regain confidence, and enjoy life again.

Whether you need a consultation, diagnosis, or surgery, our experienced orthopaedic surgeons in Ravet will guide you every step of the way, ensuring the best care and support throughout your treatment.

Our Orthopedic Services

1) Joint Replacement Surgery

At our orthopaedic hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, we specialise in advanced knee, hip, and shoulder joint replacement surgeries using minimally invasive and robotic techniques. Our expert orthopaedic surgeons ensure faster recovery, less pain, and improved mobility - helping you regain strength and confidence to live an active, pain-free life.

2) Spine Surgery & Treatment

Our spine specialists in Pune provide comprehensive care for back and neck disorders, including slipped discs, scoliosis, and spine injuries. We offer both endoscopic and minimally invasive spine surgeries, ensuring precision and faster rehabilitation. Our focus is on restoring spinal health safely and effectively for long-term comfort and flexibility.

3) Sports Injury & Arthroscopic Surgery

We offer expert sports injury treatment in Ravet for ligament tears, ACL reconstruction, and shoulder dislocations. Using arthroscopic (keyhole) surgery, our orthopaedic team ensures quicker recovery with minimal downtime. Whether you're an athlete or fitness enthusiast, we help you return to your active lifestyle stronger and pain-free.

4) Trauma & Fracture Management

Our 24/7 orthopaedic trauma centre in PCMC handles all types of fractures and complex bone injuries. With advanced surgical facilities and immediate emergency care, we ensure rapid stabilisation and recovery. Our trauma specialists provide personalised treatment plans to restore mobility and prevent long-term complications after serious injuries.

5) Paediatric Orthopaedic Care

Children require special attention for bone- and growth-related issues. Our paediatric orthopaedic doctors in Ravet treat congenital deformities, fractures, and limb-length differences with compassion and precision. Using safe, child-friendly techniques, we ensure every child receives expert orthopaedic care for healthy growth and long-term musculoskeletal wellness.

6) Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Our physiotherapy and rehabilitation centre in Pimpri Chinchwad focuses on helping patients recover strength, mobility, and balance after orthopaedic surgery or injury. With personalised exercise programmes and modern equipment, our specialists ensure faster healing, improved flexibility, and a smooth return to daily activities - all under expert orthopaedic guidance.
