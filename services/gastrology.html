Best Gastroenterology Hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad

Are you struggling with acidity, bloating, or that constant stomach pain that just won't go away? Or maybe you're looking for reliable treatment for liver, gallbladder, or intestinal problems?

Welcome to Gore Hospital — the best gastroenterology hospital in Ravet, Pimpri Chinchwad, where expert care meets modern technology.

Here, our experienced gastroenterologists in Ravet focus on understanding your problem deeply and treating it with care and precision. From routine endoscopy and advanced GI surgeries to 24x7gastro emergency care, everything you need for your digestive and liver health is available under one roof — right here in your neighbourhood.

Our Gastroenterology Services

1. Liver & Pancreas Care

Your liver works silently every day to keep your body healthy. If you're facing issues like fatty liver, hepatitis, or pancreatitis, our liver specialists in Pimpri Chinchwad can help. We use advanced liver function tests, ultrasound imaging, and biopsy to understand the exact condition and create a customised treatment plan. Our focus is not just on recovery but also on preventing future damage through proper diet and lifestyle guidance.

2. Acidity, GERD & IBS Treatment

If acidity or indigestion keeps coming back, it's time to stop guessing and get expert help. Our GERD and IBS specialists in Pimpri Chinchwad treat acidity, heartburn, and irritable bowel syndrome through a mix of medication, diet advice, and lifestyle changes. We focus on finding the root cause so you can enjoy meals again — without that burning or bloated feeling.

3. Digestive Disorder Management

Every stomach is different — and so are digestive problems. From gastritis and ulcers to constipation or diarrhoea, our gastroenterology team in Ravet takes a detailed approach to understand your symptoms. Using endoscopy, imaging, and lab tests, we identify the real cause and create a step-by-step plan for healing, nutrition, and prevention — to keep your digestive system healthy for the long run.

4. 24x7Gastro Emergency Care

Digestive emergencies can happen anytime — and waiting is not an option. Our 24x7gastro emergency department in Ravet is always ready to handle urgent cases like severe stomach pain, vomiting blood, intestinal blockage, or liver failure. With on-call specialists, ICU backup, and quick response care, we ensure you or your loved one gets immediate attention when it matters most.
